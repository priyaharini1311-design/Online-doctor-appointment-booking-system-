ONLINE DOCTOR APPOINTMENT BOOKING SYSTEM

1. PROJECT TITLE

Online Doctor Appointment Booking System

---

2. PROBLEM STATEMENT

In today's healthcare environment, many hospitals and clinics still rely on manual methods for scheduling appointments. Patients often face long waiting times, difficulty in contacting doctors, and inconvenience in managing appointments. These challenges can lead to poor patient experience and inefficient hospital operations.

The Online Doctor Appointment Booking System addresses these issues by providing a digital platform where patients can easily book, reschedule, or cancel appointments, while doctors and administrators can efficiently manage schedules and patient records.

---

3. PROJECT OBJECTIVE

The primary objectives of this project are:

- To provide an easy-to-use online appointment booking platform.
- To reduce patient waiting time and improve service quality.
- To enable doctors to manage appointments efficiently.
- To maintain patient and appointment records securely.
- To provide appointment notifications and updates.
- To improve overall hospital management and communication.

---

4. MODULE LIST

Patient Module

- User Registration and Login
- Search Doctors
- Book Appointments
- View Appointment History
- Cancel or Reschedule Appointments

Doctor Module

- Doctor Login
- Manage Profile
- View Appointments
- Update Appointment Status
- Manage Availability

Admin Module

- Manage Doctors
- Manage Patients
- Manage Appointments
- Generate Reports
- System Administration

Appointment Management Module

- Schedule Appointments
- Confirm Appointments
- Cancel Appointments
- Manage Time Slots

Notification Module

- Appointment Reminders
- Status Notifications
- Email/SMS Alerts

Database Management Module

- Store Patient Information
- Store Doctor Information
- Store Appointment Records
- Data Backup and Security
